Welcome to Syntaxr's detailed privacy policy. This policy explains how we handle data in our mobile applications, particularly in relation to our app "Cellz". We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, enjoyable experience while using our products.

At Syntaxr, we prioritize your privacy. We want to clearly state that:

Our app "Cellz" may request certain permissions to function properly. These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for data collection. For example:

We do not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising services in our apps. This ensures that no external entities have access to any data related to your use of our applications.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are designed to be suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.
